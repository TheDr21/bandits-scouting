Pitch approach
Don't give in — pitch around her with runners on, especially behind Maryanna and Tenley. She doesn't chase much and makes consistent hard contact, so middle-of-the-plate mistakes get punished. Work the edges, change speeds, and expand the zone with two strikes. With first base open, a careful walk is far better than a mistake she drives. The challenge: the bats around her are just as good, so there's no easy out to pitch toward.
