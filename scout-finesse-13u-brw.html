Emerson took a huge step forward from fall to spring: .297 → .436, with her strikeout rate dropping to 9% while her walk rate held at 22%. She led the spring team in runs scored with 49 and posted a .558 OBP. She reaches base more than half the time and almost never chases. What changed fall → spring
Average jumped 139 points. .297 → .436 on a real sample (101 spring AB) — a true adjustment, not small-sample noise.
Contact tightened. K% fell from 15% to 9%. More balls in play, less chase.
Discipline was always there. 21–22% walk rate both seasons — patient, works counts.
Elite baserunning value. 49 runs on only 19 RBI — she's getting on and scoring on the bats behind her.
