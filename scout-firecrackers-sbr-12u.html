Pitch approach
You will not strike her out — the goal is soft contact and keeping her off the bases. She makes contact on nearly everything, so chasing strikeouts is a losing game. Change speeds, move her eye level, work the corners, and avoid anything middle-middle. With first base open and the game on the line, a careful walk is a perfectly good outcome. Limit her on the bases — she scores 45 runs and is the engine of this offense.

Read on the staff
Viola W is the ace. 2.91 spring ERA, 1.45 WHIP, and she barely walks anyone (10 BB in 45.2 IP). She pounds the zone — be ready to hit early-count strikes, she won't gift free passes.
Kora R has the best swing-and-miss. 10.3 K/9 — the arm most likely to strike you out. Hunt fastballs early before she gets two strikes.
Mia B is the most hittable. 1.66 WHIP and a 5.7 K/9 — she allows contact and base traffic. If she's in, be patient and make her work.
Deep, balanced staff. Four arms between 2.91 and 3.43 ERA in spring — no obvious weak link, but also no overpowering strikeout machine. Put the ball in play and pressure the defense.
